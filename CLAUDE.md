# Project Instructions

## Language and Formatting
- Always use UK English spelling and grammar (e.g., "personalise" not "personalize", "organisation" not "organization", "colour" not "color")
- Use em dashes (—) for emphasis, not hyphens or double hyphens

## Portfolio Content Guidelines
- Keep content concise and scannable for recruiters and clients
- Focus on strategic decision-making and VP-level impact
- Show outcomes and shipped products over concepts
- Use direct, active language without unnecessary superlatives
- Turn bullet lists into flowing paragraphs for better readability
- Condense multiple sections when they become repetitive

## Case Study Format
- Use chapter-based structure (Chapter 1, 2, 3) showing progression
- 5-6 slides per case study maximum
- Each slide: visual-first with concise text
- Avoid separate title pages (modern approach)
- Show transformation: foundation → innovation → system-level change
- Final slide combines outcomes + key learning

## Project Context
This is Ivo's professional portfolio including:
- CV showcasing 10+ years design leadership experience
- Case studies for JAAQ, IKEA, NHS, and Spotify projects
- "What Sets Me Apart" document highlighting 5 key differentiators
- Focus on shipped products with major brands
